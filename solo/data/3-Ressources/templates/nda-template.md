# Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

**Between:** [Your Business Name]  
**And:** [Company Name]  
**Effective Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]

---

## 1. Purpose

The parties wish to explore a potential business relationship and, in the process, may share certain confidential information.

## 2. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" includes all non-public information, whether written or oral, relating to the disclosing party's business, including trade secrets, customer lists, and financial data.

## 3. Obligations of Receiving Party

The receiving party shall:

- Use the information only for the specified purpose.
- Maintain strict confidentiality.
- Not disclose the information to any third party without consent.

## 4. Term

This agreement shall remain in effect for a period of [2] years from the date of disclosure.
