# Positioning Statement Frameworks

Choose the framework that best fits your current brand stage.

## 1. The Moore Framework (Standard)

> **FOR** [Target Customer] **WHO** [Statement of need/opportunity], **THE** [Product Name] **IS A** [Product Category] **THAT** [Statement of Key Benefit]. **UNLIKE** [Primary Competitive Alternative], **OUR PRODUCT** [Statement of Primary Differentiation].

## 2. The Dunford Framework (Competitive)

_Focus: Contextualizing value in a crowded market._

1.  **Competitive Alternatives**: What would they do if you didn't exist?
2.  **Unique Features**: What do you have that they don't?
3.  **Value**: What is the outcome for the user?
4.  **Target Market**: Who cares most about that value?
5.  **Market Category**: What "Shelf" do you sit on?

## 3. The StoryBrand Framework (Narrative)

_Focus: The user as the hero._

1.  **The Hero**: [User]
2.  **Has a Problem**: [Pain]
3.  **And Meets a Guide**: [You/Your Product]
4.  **Who Gives them a Plan**: [The Method]
5.  **And Calls them to Action**: [The CTA]
6.  **That Ends in Success**: [Vision of the future]
7.  **And Avoids Failure**: [Pain avoided]
