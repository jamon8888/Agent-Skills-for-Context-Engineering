# The Client Relationship Architecture: Beyond a Simple CRM

For a solopreneur, every client relationship is high-stakes. This architecture moves you from a "passive database" to an "active relationship engine." Use this template to manage your most valuable business assets.

---

### 3. Lead & Pipeline Management (The Stage)

Track the lifecycle of the relationship:

- **[STAGE] Discovery**: Identifying pain points via `discovery-interview-prep`.
- **[STAGE] Proposal**: Drafting the `Triple Tier` pricing options.
- **[STAGE] Active**: Managing the current `Walking Skeleton` or `V1.0` build.
- **[STAGE] Retention**: Monthly advisory and strategic check-ins.
- **[STAGE] Sunset**: Professional offboarding (Ref: `eol-message`).

---

### 4. Strategic Memory (Discovery Log)

_Capture high-signal quotes that can be used for future sales._

- **The "Aha" Moment**: "[Quote from client about when the value became clear]"
- **The "Unmet Need"**: "[Something they complained about that isn't in scope yet]"
- **The "Champion"**: [Who inside the company is your biggest advocate?]

---

## 5. Implementation Workflow

1.  **Onboard**: Trigger the `client-management` skill to create the master file.
2.  **Audit**: Run a "Health Check" session every Friday during your `Weekly Beat`.
3.  **Propose**: Use the "Unmet Needs" from Section 4 to pitch your next `Project` or `Subscription`.
