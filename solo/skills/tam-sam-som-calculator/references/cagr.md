# CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate) Calculator

A simple way to measure your growth over multiple years.

## 1. The Formula

> **CAGR = [(Ending Value / Beginning Value)^(1 / Number of Years)] - 1**

## 2. Why it matters

Year-over-year growth can be volatile. CAGR smoothes out the bumps to give you a clear trend line for your business health.

## 3. Targets

- **Steady State**: 5-10%.
- **High Growth**: 20-50%+.
- **Hyper-Growth**: >100% (Usual for early-stage micro-SaaS).
