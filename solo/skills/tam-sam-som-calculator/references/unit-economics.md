# Unit Economics for Solopreneurs

How to ensure every client or user is actually profitable.

## 1. CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)

- **Formula**: (Marketing Spend + Your Time Cost) / New Customers.
- **Solo Tip**: Value your time at your "Goal Hourly Rate" when calculating CAC.

## 2. LTV (Lifetime Value)

- **Formula**: Average Revenue per User \* Average Lifespan.

## 3. The Magic Ratio (LTV:CAC)

- **Target**: >3:1.
- **Risk**: If ratio is <1:1, you are paying to work. Stop and PIVOT.

## 4. Churn

- % of users who leave each month. Use this to predict when your business "Leaks" money.
