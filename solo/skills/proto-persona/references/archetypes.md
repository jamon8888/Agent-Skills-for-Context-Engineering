# Proto-Persona Archetypes

A library of starting-point personas for various solopreneur products.

## 1. The Time-Starved Executive

- **Context**: Corporate background, now consulting.
- **Pain**: Busy work, meeting fatigue, lack of deep focus.
- **Motivation**: To automate the boring and win back 10 hours/week.

## 2. The Aspiring Creator

- **Context**: Building a side-hustle while working 9-5.
- **Pain**: Consistency, "Imposter Syndrome," lack of technical skills.
- **Motivation**: To publish every week and reach 1k subs.

## 3. The Local Specialist

- **Context**: Independent electrician, florist, or yoga teacher.
- **Pain**: Booking management, invoicing, high-friction client communication.
- **Motivation**: Professional-looking business without a large agency budget.
