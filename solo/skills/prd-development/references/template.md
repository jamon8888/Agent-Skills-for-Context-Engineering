# PRD Template (Product Requirements Document)

Use this template to produce a structured and actionable specification document.

## 1. Executive Summary

- One-paragraph overview (problem + solution + impact).

## 2. Problem Statement

- Who has this problem?
- What is the problem?
- Why is it painful?
- Evidence (customer quotes, data, research).

## 3. Target Users & Personas

- Primary persona(s).
- Secondary persona(s).
- Jobs-to-be-done (JTBD).

## 4. Strategic Context

- Business goals (OKRs).
- Market opportunity (TAM/SAM/SOM).
- Competitive landscape.
- Why now?

## 5. Solution Overview

- High-level description.
- User flows or wireframes.
- Key features.

## 6. Success Metrics (KPIs)

- Primary indicator (what we are optimizing).
- Secondary indicators.
- Targets (current -> goal).

## 7. User Stories & Requirements

- Epic hypothesis.
- User stories with acceptance criteria.
- Edge cases and constraints.

## 8. Out of Scope

- What we are NOT building (and why).

## 9. Dependencies & Risks

- Technical dependencies.
- External dependencies (integrations, partnerships).
- Risks and mitigation plans.

## 10. Open Questions

- Unresolved decisions.
- Areas requiring more discovery.
