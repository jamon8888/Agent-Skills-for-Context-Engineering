# Email Sequence Structures (The Blueprints)

Use these architectures to build your campaigns.

## 1. The "Soap Opera" Sequence (5 Days)

_Best for: Welcome Series / Indoctrination._
_Goal: Hook them with a story, then sell the solution._

- **Day 1: Set the Stage.** High drama. Start in the middle of the trouble. "I was $50k in debt..."
- **Day 2: The Backstory.** How did you get there? "It started when..."
- **Day 3: The Epiphany.** The discovery of the mechanism. "Then I realized..."
- **Day 4: The Hidden Benefits.** Why this mechanism is different.
- **Day 5: The Urgency/CTA.** "If you want this result..."

## 2. The "Product Launch Formula" (PLF) Lite

_Best for: Selling a Course or High-Ticket Item._
_Goal: Educate first, sell second._

- **Email 1: The Opportunity.** "There is a new way to [Result]." (No pitch).
- **Email 2: The Transformation.** "Here is what happens when you switch." (Case study).
- **Email 3: The Ownership Experience.** "Imagine waking up and..." (Future pacing).
- **Email 4: The Offer.** Open Cart. Full details.
- **Email 5: The Logic.** FAQ and Guarantee.
- **Email 6: The Scarcity.** Closing soon.

## 3. The "Dean Jackson" 9-Word Email

_Best for: Reactivating dead leads._
_Goal: Start a conversation._

- **Subject**: [First Name]
- **Body**: "Are you still looking for help with [Problem]?"
- _That's it. Nothing else._

## 4. The "Discount Ladder" (Abandoned Cart)

_Best for: E-commerce or Checkout Abandonment._

- **Hour 1**: "Did life get in the way?" (Helpful, store link).
- **Hour 24**: "I saved your cart." (Social proof).
- **Hour 48**: "Last chance + 10% off." (Scarcity).
