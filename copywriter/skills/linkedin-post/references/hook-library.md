# The LinkedIn Hook Library: 30+ Scroll-Stop Formulas

The "Hook" is the first 1-2 lines of your LinkedIn post. It determines whether a user clicks "See More" or continues scrolling. This vault provides the psychological blueprints for hooks that drive "Dwell Time" and engagement.

---

## ðŸ› 1. The 3 Laws of the LinkedIn Hook

1.  **The "See More" Gap**: The first 140 characters must create a narrative gap that can only be filled by clicking the expand button.
2.  **The No-Fluff Rule**: No "I'm excited to share..." or "In today's landscape..." Start mid-action.
3.  **The Formatting Rule**: Use a maximum of 2 lines before a blank line to ensure the hook sits above the fold.

## ðŸ§¬ 2. The Hook Formula Vault

### Category A: The "Contrarian Leader" (High Authority)

_Goal: To challenge the status quo and build debate._

- **Formula**: "[Commonly Accepted Truth] is the reason you're failing at [Result]."
  - _Example_: "Posting daily is the reason your LinkedIn reach is dying."
- **Formula**: "Stop [Action] if you want [Result]. Do this instead:"
- **Formula**: "I disagree with [Industry Leader/Standard]. Here is why [Aha! Moment]:"
- **Formula**: "X is dead. Y is the only thing that matters in 2026."
- **Formula**: "Most people think [X] is hard. It's actually [Simple Truth]."

### Category B: The "Vulnerable Journey" (High Trust)

_Goal: To build empathy and personal brand depth._

- **Formula**: "Last [Time Period], I almost quit [Project Name]. Here's what saved me:"
- **Formula**: "My $10k mistake. (And how you can avoid it)."
- **Formula**: "I spent [Specific Time] doing [Action]. I learned 3 things that changed everything:"
- **Formula**: "The hardest conversation I've had as a solopreneur happened yesterday."
- **Formula**: "I was terrified to hit publish on this. But here it is:"

### Category C: The "Value-First" (High Utility)

_Goal: To provide immediate, actionable knowledge._

- **Formula**: "I found a [Tool/Method] that shaves [X hours] off my [Workflow]."
- **Formula**: "The 1-page roadmap to [Result] (No fluff):"
- **Formula**: "If I had to start [Business Type] from $0 again, I'd do these 5 things:"
- **Formula**: "7 tools that feel illegal for a one-man marketing team:"
- **Formula**: "[Topic] is complicated. Here is a 30-second summary for busy people:"

### Category D: The "Evidence-Based" (High Credibility)

_Goal: To prove results and technical competence._

- **Formula**: "From [Metric A] to [Metric B] in [Specific Time]. No ads."
- **Formula**: "I audited [Specific Brand] and found the one thing holding them back."
- **Formula**: "The data is in: [Specific Stat] proves that [Hypothesis] is true."
- **Formula**: "3 case studies that show why [Method] beats [Standard]."
- **Formula**: "How I moved my [Metric] by [Percentage] using a simple PARA change."

## ðŸ§ª 5. The Active Post-Audit

Before you post, check your hook against these 5 filters:

- [ ] **Character Count**: Is the "meat" visible before the 140th character?
- [ ] **Curiosity Gap**: Does it ask a question the user _must_ know the answer to?
- [ ] **First-Person**: Does it use "I" or "My" to signal experience? (Ref: `voice-dna.json`)
- [ ] **Audience Match**: Does it use the specific jargon from `icp.json`?
- [ ] **AntiSlop**: Is it free of "leverage," "impactful," and "synergy"?
