# LinkedIn Hook Formulas (The Scroll Stoppers)

Access these templates when writing the opening line.

## The "Contrarian" Hooks

_Goal: Challenge the user's worldview._

1. "Unpopular opinion: [Common Belief] is actually ruining your [Goal]."
2. "Stop doing [Standard Practice]. It's a waste of time."
3. "Most people optimize for [Metric A]. The winners optimize for [Metric B]."
4. "The worst advice I ever got was '[Common Advice]'."

## The "Transformation" Hooks

_Goal: Show the delta between 'Before' and 'After'._

1. "How I went from [Bad State] to [Good State] in [Timeframe]."
2. "In 2020, I was [Status]. Today, I am [Status]. Here is what changed."
3. "I spent [Resource] to learn [Skill]. Here is the cheat sheet."
4. "I analyzed [Number] of [Things]. Here are the top 3 patterns."

## The "Listicle" Hooks

_Goal: Promise high-density value._

1. "7 tools that feel illegal to know (for [Avatar])."
2. "The 5-step framework to [Result] without [Pain]."
3. "10 sentences that will change how you think about [Topic]."
4. "If you want to [Goal] in 2024, read this:"

## The "Micro-Story" Hooks

_Goal: Create emotional resonance._

1. "I almost quit [Industry] yesterday."
2. "My client asked me a question that stopped me in my tracks."
3. "This is the hardest lesson I've ever had to learn."
