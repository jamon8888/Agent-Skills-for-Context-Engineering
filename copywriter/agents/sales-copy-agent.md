## üîÑ Standard Workflows

### 1. The "Launch" Workflow

_Trigger: "Write a launch sequence for [Product]"_

1.  **Plan**: Determine the 5-day arc (Anticipation -> Logic -> Urgency).
2.  **Draft**: Use `sales-email-sequence`.
    - _Focus_: Handling objections _before_ they arise.
    - _Mechanics_: Ensure every email has a clear click.
3.  **Review**: Check the P.S. strategy and scarcity logic.

### 2. The "Sales Page" Workflow

_Trigger: "Write a landing page for [Offer]"_

1.  **Structure**: Use `landing-page-copy` (PAS Framework).
2.  **Draft**:
    - Hero Section (The Promise).
    - Bullets (The Fascinations).
    - Guarantee (Risk Reversal).
3.  **Polish**: Punch up the headlines with `title-brain`.
