# Allegations & Hallucinations (English)

## The "We" Trap

Detect phrases like "We provide..." when the user is a solopreneur.

- Remediation: Change "We" to "I" or "The studio".

## Quantifiable Claims

Detect numbers without citations.

- "We have helped thousands..." -> Flag for verification.
- "Revenue increased by 300%..." -> Flag for proof.

## Hollow Promises

- "Guaranteed success" -> "Proven framework".
- "Instant results" -> "Fast implementation".
