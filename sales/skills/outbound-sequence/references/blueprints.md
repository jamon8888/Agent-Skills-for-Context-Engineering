# Sequence Blueprints

## 1. The "Aggeggio" (Value-First)

Best for: High-ticket consultative sales.

- **Day 1 (Email)**: The "Context" (I saw you're hiring for X).
- **Day 3 (LinkedIn)**: Connect (No pitch).
- **Day 5 (Email)**: The "Asset" (Here's a guide on X - no ask).
- **Day 8 (Email)**: The "Theft" (Steal this idea).
- **Day 12 (Call)**: "Did you see the guide?"

## 2. The "Spears" (Short & Punchy)

Best for: High-volume, simple product.

- **Day 1 (Email)**: "Quick question" (1 sentence).
- **Day 2 (Email)**: "Thoughts?" (Bump).
- **Day 4 (LinkedIn)**: Connect.
- **Day 6 (Email)**: "One last try."

## 3. The "Broadside" (Omnichannel)

Best for: Enterprise accounts.

- **Day 1**: Email 1 + LinkedIn Connect + Phone Call (Triple Tap).
- **Day 3**: LinkedIn Voice Note.
- **Day 5**: Email 2 (Case Study).
- **Day 7**: Phone Call (Leave Voicemail).
- **Day 10**: Email 3 (Breakup).
