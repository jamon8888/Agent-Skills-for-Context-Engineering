# Email Templates (High Conversion)

## 1. The "Observer" (Cold Opener)

**Subject**: [problem] at [company]
**Body**:

> Hi [Name],
>
> Saw you're hiring for [Role]. Usually that means [Problem X] is becoming a headache.
>
> We help teams like [Competitor] fix [Problem X] without [Common Pain].
>
> Open to seeing how they did it?
>
> Best,
> [You]

## 2. The "Asset" (Value Give)

**Subject**: idea for [company]
**Body**:

> Hi [Name],
>
> Wrote a quick checklist on how to solve [Problem X]. It's based on what we saw at [Client A].
>
> No pitch, just thought it might help with the [Role] search.
>
> [Link to Asset]
>
> Cheers,
> [You]

## 3. The "Breakup" (The last Hail Mary)

**Subject**: permission to close?
**Body**:

> Hi [Name],
>
> Assuming [Problem X] isn't a priority right now, so I'm crossing this off my list.
>
> If I'm wrong and you still want to fix [Problem Y], let me know.
>
> Best,
> [You]
