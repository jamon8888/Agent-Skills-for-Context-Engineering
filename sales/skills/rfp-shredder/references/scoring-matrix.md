# RFP Scoring Matrix

Use this matrix to calculate the "Fit Score" (0-100).

## 1. Technical Fit (Max 40 pts)

- [ ] **Core Solution (20 pts)**: Do we have the product TODAY? (Yes=20, Partial=10, Build=0)
- [ ] **Integrations (10 pts)**: Do we have the connectors? (Native=10, API=5, Custom=0)
- [ ] **Security/Compliance (10 pts)**: Do we meet the certs (SOC2, ISO)? (Yes=10, No=KILL DEAL)

## 2. Business Fit (Max 30 pts)

- [ ] **Budget (10 pts)**: Is budget stated and adequate? (Yes=10, Unknown=5, Low=0)
- [ ] **Timeline (10 pts)**: Is the timeline realistic? (>4 weeks=10, <2 weeks=0)
- [ ] **Competitors (10 pts)**: Are we the "Column A" or "Column F"? (Incumbent=10, Invited early=5, Blind RFP=0)

## 3. Strategic Fit (Max 30 pts)

- [ ] **ICP Alignment (15 pts)**: Is this our ideal customer size/industry? (Perfect=15, Okay=5, Wrong=0)
- [ ] **Relationship (15 pts)**: Do we know the buyer? (Champion=15, Coach=10, Stranger=0)

## Scoring Guide

- **> 70**: **GO**. Invest resources.
- **50 - 70**: **Review**. Need Executive Sponsor.
- **< 50**: **NO-GO**. Send polite decline.
