# ICP Examples

## Example 1: B2B SaaS (The "Bootstrapped Founder")

**Demographics:**

- Age: 30-45
- Role: Founder/CEO
- Company: Bootstrapped SaaS, $10k-$50k MRR
- Team: Remote, <10 people

**Pain Points:**

- Churn reduction
- Hiring first key employees
- Scaling without VC money

**Goals:**

- Hit $1M ARR
- maintain freedom

## Example 2: Service Business (The "Agency Owner")

**Demographics:**

- Age: 25-40
- Role: Agency Owner
- Services: SEO, Design, Dev
- Revenue: $20k-$100k/mo

**Pain Points:**

- Feast/Famine cycle
- Client management issues
- Scope creep

**Goals:**

- Productize services
- Remove themselves from delivery
