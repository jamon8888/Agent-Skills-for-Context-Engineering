# Deep Dive Questions

Use these if the user gives vague answers.

**If they say "Small Business Owner":**

- "Retail or Digital?"
- "Local or Global?"
- "Family business or Startup?"

**If they say "They want to grow":**

- "Grow revenue or headcount?"
- "Grow fast (VC) or slow (Profit)?"

**If they say "They are stressed":**

- "About money or time?"
- "About employees or customers?"
