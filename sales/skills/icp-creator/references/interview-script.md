# 7-Phase Interview Script

Use this script to guide the user through the ICP creation process.

## Phase 1: Demographics

"Let's start with the basics. Who are they demographically?"

- Age range?
- Location?
- Income level?
- Education?

## Phase 2: Professional Profile

"What do they do for work?"

- Job title?
- Industry?
- Company size?
- Years of experience?

## Phase 3: Psychographics

"Let's get inside their head. How do they think?"

- Values?
- Beliefs about work/life?
- Personality traits?
- Motivations?

## Phase 4: Problems & Pain Points

"What keeps them up at night? This is critical."

- Top 3 frustrations?
- Active problems?
- Failed solutions?

## Phase 5: Goals & Desires

"Where do they want to go?"

- Short-term goals (6-12 months)?
- Long-term goals (3-5 years)?
- Definition of success?
- Who do they aspire to be?

## Phase 6: Language Patterns

"How do they talk?"

- Frequent words/phrases?
- Common questions?
- Jargon?
- How do they describe their problems?

## Phase 7: Content & Buying Behavior

"Where do they hang out and how do they buy?"

- Online hangouts?
- Content consumed?
- Buying process?
- Purchase triggers?
